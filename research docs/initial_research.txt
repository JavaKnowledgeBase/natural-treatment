Summary

Core Findings & Medical Logic
The Diagnostic Formula: Modern medical data decomposes a traditional disease diagnosis into a strict relational formula:
\(\text{Cause\ (Etiology)}+\text{Symptom/Sign\ (Phenotype)}=\text{Disease\ Label}\)
By removing the arbitrary "Disease" layer, your system focuses entirely on tracing how physical and mental symptoms are triggered by molecular deviations.
Biochemical Grounding: At the cellular level, symptoms emerge because the body drifts past optimal concentration thresholds into two states:
Deficiency: A lack of critical atoms or nutrients required to drive vital metabolic pathways (e.g., lack of Iron causing cellular hypoxia and chronic fatigue).
Excess (Toxicity): The intrusion of heavy metals, environmental poisons, or metabolic waste that destroys cell walls or blocks critical enzyme function (e.g., Lead poisoning blocking nerve signals).
The Botanical Mechanism: Natural plants and herbs resolve these imbalances via two core evolutionary traits:
Bioaccumulation: Concentrating dense, highly bioavailable vitamins and trace minerals from the earth to instantly reverse human deficiencies.
Secondary Metabolites: Manufacturing specialized compounds (like flavonoids and polyphenols) that actively act as chelators to bind and clear toxic excesses.
Architectural Design & Tech Stack
To translate these findings into a functional application, the system uses a cross-layered tech stack:
Data Layer (The Unified Ecosystem): Rather than scraping unstructured consumer blogs, the architecture integrates production-ready scientific endpoints including IMPPAT and KNApSAcK (for botanical chemical profiling), ChEBI and PubChem (for validating molecular compounds), and the Comparative Toxicogenomics Database (CTD) (for linking compounds to physical phenotypes).
Database Infrastructure: A customized relational schema (PostgreSQL) was established to create clean multi-junction connections between plants, concentrations (mg/kg), and active mechanisms of action (e.g., heavy metal chelation), fully indexed for instant symptom-based querying.
The High-Confidence Algorithmic Engine: To protect patient safety and provide accurate data outputs, the system utilizes a mathematical Confidence Score (CS):
\(\text{Confidence\ Score}=(\text{Concentration\ Factor}\times 0.4)+(\text{Evidence\ Level\ Factor}\times 0.6)\)
This engine automatically deprioritizes weak cellular petri-dish data and highlights herbs with massive natural concentrations backed by peer-reviewed human clinical trials.
